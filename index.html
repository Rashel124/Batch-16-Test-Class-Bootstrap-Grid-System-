What is Digital Marketing? Digital marketing is the practice of promoting products, services, or brands using digital platforms, channels, and technologies. It encompasses a variety of strategies aimed at connecting businesses with their target audiences online, where they spend much of their time. Unlike traditional marketing methods, digital marketing provides real-time engagement, precise targeting, and measurable results.
Social Media Marketing (SMM): Using platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn to interact with users, build brand awareness, and run paid ad campaigns.
Email Marketing: Sending targeted and personalized emails to nurture leads and build customer relationships.
Search Engine Optimization (SEO): The process of optimizing websites to rank higher in search engine results and attract organic traffic.
